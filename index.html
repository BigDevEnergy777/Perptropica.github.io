How Zero Fees Work

Zero Fees Flow

All trading costs are covered by $TROPIC airdrops. Users submit trades via the frontend, relayers execute them on-chain, and FeeReimburser reimburses relayers using allocated $TROPIC. Wallets are capped for 15 free trades per week. Governance can adjust emissions, free trade caps, and deflationary mechanisms. The system ensures full transparency and sustainable zero-fee trading.
