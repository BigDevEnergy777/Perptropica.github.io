How Zero Fees Work

PerpTropica removes trading fees for users by reimbursing execution costs via $TROPIC. Meta-transactions are executed by relayers, and all costs are transparent and auditable on-chain.

Relayers submit trades and are reimbursed from weekly airdrops. Governance ensures sustainable emissions and adjustable free trade caps.
