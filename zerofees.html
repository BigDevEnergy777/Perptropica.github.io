How Zero Fees Work

PerpTropica uses a native token, $TROPIC, to cover all trading fees. Relayers execute trades and receive reimbursement from fee wallets funded by weekly airdrops. This ensures traders focus on strategy without worrying about fees.

Key components: AirdropManager allocates weekly $TROPIC, FeeReimburser ensures relayers are paid, and PerpEngine handles margin, funding, and liquidations.

Governance allows emission adjustments, free trade caps, and deflationary sinks. Transparent audits and on-chain metrics ensure security and sustainability.
