About PerpTropica

PerpTropica is a community-first DeFi platform for fee-free perpetual trading. Our mission is to lower trading costs and enable strategies previously uneconomical due to fees.

Founded by DeFi enthusiasts, our roadmap includes research, core contract deployment, relayer infrastructure, testnet launches, audits, and mainnet with community airdrops.

We encourage contributors across smart contract development, frontend design, relayer systems, and community building. Stay updated through X (Twitter).
